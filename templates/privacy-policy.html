Privacy Policy

At Fuel Hound, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

Data Collection

We collect the following data: your postcode, which is used to find the cheapest fuel prices nearby.

How We Use Your Data

Your postcode is used only for the purpose of providing fuel prices based on your location.

Browser Data and Session Management

While we strive to ensure that your data is securely managed on our platform, please be aware that your browser may cache pages, session data, or form inputs. This could cause previous search results to persist or reappear upon refreshing the page. This behavior is specific to your browser and outside of our control.

We recommend that you refresh the page or use an Incognito/Private mode in your browser to ensure that no cached or old data is displayed. Fuel Hound is not responsible for any issues arising from browser caching or session data persistence.

Data Privacy

The data you enter, such as your postcode, is used solely for the purpose of providing fuel price information relevant to your location. Rest assured, the data you submit is only visible to you during your session and is not shared with other users. Your personal information remains private, and no data is leaked or shared across sessions.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you, as well as to request its deletion or correction. Please contact us for more information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at: support@fuelhound.com
